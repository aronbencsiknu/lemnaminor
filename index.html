About us

LemnaMinor assists its partners in the procurement of laboratory consumables and reagents, as well as in the use of related services, primarily in the fields of life sciences and molecular research, biotechnology, the pharmaceutical industry, and clinical and veterinary laboratory diagnostics. We aim to serve the work of our clients to the best of our ability and make it more efficient and economical by providing the necessary materials and tools in a well-organized manner.

Services

Feel free to contact us with individual needs and requests, as we experience it as an exciting and useful task to find a solution in consultation with our partners, adapted to the given possibilities and ideas.

Procurement - distribution

Our product range includes laboratory plastic and glassware consumables, general and molecular biological reagents, antibodies, ELISA kits, cell and tissue culture-related materials, as well as small laboratory instruments.

Consultancy

This includes the assessment of individual laboratory needs, as well as the preparation of procurement needs for public procurement.

Protein synthesis

Through our partners, we can also help our customers with the on-demand protein synthesis service. In this regard, we welcome your inquiry at one of the contact details provided on the website.

Antibody manufacuring

The services provided by our company include the production of antibodies to meet individual needs. In this regard, we welcome your inquiry at one of the contact details provided on the website.

Products

The focus of our activity is to supply the operation of laboratories with high-quality, reliable, but at the same time value-for-money products. Our product range includes laboratory plastic and glassware consumables, general and molecular biological reagents, antibodies, ELISA kits, cell and tissue culture-related materials, as well as small laboratory instruments.
